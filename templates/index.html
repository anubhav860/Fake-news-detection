ABOUT WEBSITE

Fake news detection website is used to detect whether the given news is real or fake We aim to use a corpus of labeled real and fake new articles to build a classifier that can make decisions about information based on the content from the corpus. The model will focus on identifying fake news sources, based on multiple articles originating from a source. Once a source is labeled as a producer of fake news, we can predict with high confidence that any future articles from that source will also be fake news.Verifying the authenticity of news needs to have prime importance though a difficult task. This helps to eliminate the rumors from spreading through social platforms
